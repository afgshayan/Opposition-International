Program Overview

The Global Oppositions Index is Opposition International's flagship data program — a comprehensive, publicly accessible resource that tracks and analyzes the state of opposition politics in over 30 countries worldwide.

By compiling data on opposition parties, election results, the legal environment for political opposition, parliamentary representation, and public opinion, the Index provides researchers, policymakers, journalists, and democracy advocates with the tools they need to understand and support democratic opposition movements globally.

This is more than a dataset — it's a tool for democracy. When citizens, parties, and international organizations have access to reliable, comprehensive data on the state of opposition politics, they can make better decisions, craft more effective strategies, and build stronger democratic institutions.

Data Categories

Opposition Parties

Profiles of major opposition parties including leadership, platform, membership, and organizational strength.

Elections & Results

Comprehensive election data including vote shares, seat allocations, and trend analysis over multiple cycles.

Legal Environment

Analysis of legal frameworks governing political opposition, party registration, and political freedoms.

Parliament & Public Opinion

Parliamentary representation data and public opinion surveys on democratic satisfaction and opposition support.

Methodology & Data Sources

V-Dem Institute

Varieties of Democracy data on democratic indicators, political participation, and institutional quality across countries.

Freedom House

Freedom in the World scores and political rights assessments integrated into our democratic environment analysis.

National Election Commissions

Official election results sourced directly from national electoral bodies for maximum accuracy and reliability.

Interactive Features (Planned)

An interactive web dashboard allowing users to explore data by country, party, and indicator — with comparison tools and downloadable datasets.
