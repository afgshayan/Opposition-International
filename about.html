Our Mission

Leveling the Playing Field

Opposition International is a two year-old non-profit providing information and news about, and advocacy and training for, opposition political parties worldwide.

Home base is Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Our most active chapters are in Dhaka (Bangladesh), Rangoon (Myanmar), Phnom Penh (Cambodia), Kampala (Uganda), Johannesburg (South Africa), Tbilisi (Georgia), Bogota (Colombia), São Paulo (Brazil), and San José (Costa Rica).

Opposition International looks to level the playing field when incumbent governments give themselves unfair advantages. On the whole, however, we stress the positive in the Government/Opposition relationship.
