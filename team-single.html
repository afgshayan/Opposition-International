About Owen

Owen Lippert founded Opposition International in 2024 with a clear mission: to provide information, advocacy, and training for opposition political parties worldwide. With a background in political science and public policy, Owen has spent his career at the intersection of democratic governance and practical politics.

His work focuses on helping opposition parties build organizational capacity, develop effective policy platforms, and engage citizens in the democratic process. Owen believes that healthy democracies require strong, well-resourced opposition movements that can hold governments accountable and offer voters genuine alternatives.

Currently on field assignment in the Philippines, Owen continues to expand OI's reach across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, building partnerships with local democratic movements and providing hands-on training and support to emerging opposition leaders.
