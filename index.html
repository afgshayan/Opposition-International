Contact Us

We are a non-profit providing information and news about, and advocacy and training for, opposition political parties worldwide. We look to level the playing field when incumbent governments give themselves unfair advantages, while stressing the positive in the Government/Opposition relationship.

100% of your donation goes directly to our mission — funding research publications, training programs for opposition party members, the Global Oppositions Index, and field operations in our chapter cities around the world.

Our home base is Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Our most active chapters are in Dhaka (Bangladesh), Rangoon (Myanmar), Phnom Penh (Cambodia), Kampala (Uganda), Johannesburg (South Africa), Tbilisi (Georgia), Bogota (Colombia), São Paulo (Brazil), and San José (Costa Rica).

You can participate in our research surveys, volunteer your expertise in political science or campaign strategy, contribute financially, or join one of our local chapters. Visit our contact page to learn about current opportunities.
