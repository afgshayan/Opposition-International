Who We Are

A Global Force for Democratic Opposition

Opposition International is dedicated to providing information, advocacy, and training for opposition political parties worldwide. Founded in 2024, we believe that strong democratic opposition is essential for accountable governance and citizen empowerment.

Global Oppositions Index

Comprehensive data on opposition parties and democratic indicators across 30+ countries.

Milestones That Matter

Key moments in our mission to strengthen democratic opposition.

Opposition International Founded

Established in Toronto by Owen Lippert to support opposition political parties worldwide.

Flagship data program launched, tracking opposition parties and democratic indicators across 30+ countries.
